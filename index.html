Features

  • The app asks you for a route or detects your location, and shows useful driving stop locations like rest areas, fuel stations, restaurants, free campgrounds, overnight parking, and a lot more.

    SmartStops works hands-free. There's no need to take your hands off the wheel or eyes off the road to check maps for stop locations. Handling and reading maps while driving is a significant dangerous distraction, even with a passenger providing directions.

     Each time you take your hands off the wheel or eyes off the road, even for seconds, you putting your life and the lives of others in danger.

    With SmaertStops it takes less than a 1/4 second to recognize upcoming stops on your device, a safe technology first used in fighter jets.
  • We all have been in a situation we had to stop our vehicle, NOW... We need a restroom, someone is sick, the baby needs a diaper change, the driver is feeling drowsy and needs to rest. This is stressful when you do not know when the next stop is coming up, or by the time you notice them, it is upon you and often much too dangerous to slow down and pull over that fast.

    With SmartStops you always know when the next stop is coming up WELL BEFORE you get to it - giving you plenty of time to adjust your driving speed and lane positioning to pull safely off the road.

    It usually is not advisable to just pull off to the side of the road to sleep, yet there may be times when it is better to pull off the road and nap than to continue driving and possibly causing an accident.
